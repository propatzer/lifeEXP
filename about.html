What is expStudy?

expStudy is a website that allows our users to use gamified study sessions to help them study more efficiently.

Phase One

expStudy is currently in phase 1 of development. In this phase, users compete against themselves to gain exp by completeing tasks. These features can be accessed right now by clicking "Dash" in the header.

Phase Two

Phase two will add a leaderboard. Users will be able to compete against each other on both a global leaderboard and also. Users will also be able to log in and save their progress. These changes are projected to come into affect by late January 2023.

Phase Three

Phase three will allow users to compete in realtime against up to 4 friends in different battle modes. These changes are expected to come into affect by march 2023.
